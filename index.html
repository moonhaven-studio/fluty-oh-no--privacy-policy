Privacy Policy — Fluty Oh No!

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Moonhaven Studio ("we," "us," or "our") handles data related to your use of the Fluty Oh No! mobile game (the "App"). By downloading or playing the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope

The App is a casual arcade game. We do not require a separate account creation to play. However, the App uses Google Play Games Services to provide leaderboards and other social gaming features. When you sign in through Google Play Games, we receive basic profile information associated with your Google account.

2. Data we may collect

Depending on which integrations are active, we may process the following types of information:

2.1. Information collected automatically

  • Usage and diagnostics data: in-app events (e.g., screens viewed, session duration), error logs, performance and stability data.
  • Device/technical identifiers: technical identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID where applicable), device model, OS version, language, time zone, IP address, and app installation identifiers.
  • Advertising data: information needed to serve, cap frequency, and measure ad performance (including the Advertising ID where applicable).

2.2. Information from Google Play Games Services

When you sign in via Google Play Games, we may receive:

  • Player profile information: your Google Play Games display name and player ID.
  • Gameplay data: scores submitted to leaderboards and, in the future, achievement progress.

This data is managed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How we use data

  • To operate the App and maintain stability (bug fixes, performance improvements).
  • To understand aggregated App usage (metrics and analytics) and improve the experience.
  • To display and measure ads.
  • To provide leaderboards and social gaming features through Google Play Games Services.
  • To prevent fraud/abuse and protect the security of the App.

You acknowledge that we are not liable when a third-party service uses information you provide to them.

We retain data for as long as necessary to operate the App and fulfill the purposes described above (e.g., diagnostics, analytics, and leaderboards), subject to technical and legal requirements. Aggregated data may be retained longer.

6. Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or information about how data is processed and shared.

Data deletion

You may request the deletion of any personal data associated with you by contacting us at hello.moonhaven@gmail.com. We will process your request and delete the applicable data within a reasonable time frame, subject to any legal obligations that may require us to retain certain information.

Advertising ID

An Advertising ID is a unique identifier assigned to your device that allows advertisers to provide relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can reset your Advertising ID at any time via your device settings, which will disassociate your device from ad targeting profiles based on the previous identifier.

On Android, go to Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID.
